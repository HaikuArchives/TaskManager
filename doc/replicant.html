Replicants

Replicants are a very useful feature of the BeOS, but the aren't well known. Therefore I'll give you a short introduction.

  1. Select "Show replicants" in you Be menu. This will show little handles on every replicantable view.
  2. Now you can drag a replcatable view into a different application. Simply click on its handle and begin to drag. Drop the view on a replicant accepting application, like the desktop. This will create a copy of that view in the destination application.
  3. To get rid of a replicated view, simply right-click on its handle and select "Delete".
